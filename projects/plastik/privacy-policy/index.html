Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 January 2026

Plastik is built to minimise the data we collect. This policy explains what we process, why, and how you can exercise your rights.

Information we may process

  • Account: email and optional name to identify your profile.
  • App usage: basic events (session started/completed, duration) to improve stability and quality.
  • Optional content: notes or mood states you choose to add to your practices.
  • Device data: minimal technical information (device model, OS version) to diagnose issues.

How we use your information

  • To provide and personalise the service and your routines.
  • To improve stability, performance, and safety of the app.
  • To communicate material changes or support updates.
  • To comply with legal and security requirements.

How we protect your data

  • We store and transmit information using industry-standard encryption.
  • Internal access is limited to authorised, audited roles.
  • We retain data only for the time needed to meet the purposes above.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, objection, or portability by emailing privacy@tagora.tech. We will respond within reasonable timeframes under applicable law.

Location and transfers

Your data may be processed in the EU or other countries with equivalent safeguards. When we use external providers, we require data protection terms and limit processing to what is necessary.

Cookies and analytics

Plastik does not use advertising cookies. We may use aggregated analytics to understand feature usage without identifying individual users.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@tagora.tech.
