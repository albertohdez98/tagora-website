How Plastik works

Select how you feel, receive a short routine, and complete it. The app removes noise: focus on guidance, breathing, and small cognitive cues. Set gentle reminders and log only what is useful.

  • Library of guided and silent practices.
  • Light reminders that do not break your flow.
  • Summarised history: trends, not invasive metrics.
  • Offline ready: works in airplane mode so you can truly disconnect.
