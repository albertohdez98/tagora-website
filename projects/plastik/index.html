How Plastik works

Pick a mode: Quick Reset (1 min) for immediate grounding, or Daily Reset (6 min) to strengthen mental hygiene. Each session combines exercises across cognitive areas without adding addictive mechanics.

  • Exercises spanning focus, breathing, memory, and awareness—no gamified loops.
  • Audio guidance kept minimal; visuals stay calm to avoid extra stimulation.
  • Optional reminders tuned for low intrusion.
  • Offline ready: use Quick or Daily Reset even in airplane mode.
