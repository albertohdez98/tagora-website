Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how routit (“the App”, “we”, “our”) handles information when you use the application. routit is developed and operated by Tagora (contact@tagora.tech).

1. Overview

routit is a route generation app for runners and walkers. The App functions without requiring an account. Login is optional and provides additional convenience, not core functionality.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 When Using the App Without Login

When you use routit without logging in, we do not collect or store any personal data. Your generated routes, preferences, and activity remain entirely stored locally on your device.

2.2 When Using Optional Login

If you choose to log in, we collect the following limited information:

  • Email address
  • UID (a unique ID assigned by the authentication provider)

No routes, activity data, preferences, or location history are sent to our servers.

2.3 Location Data

routit uses your device’s location to generate routes. This location data:

  • remains on your device,
  • is not transmitted to our servers,
  • is not stored after generating the route.

3. How We Use Information

If you log in, your email and UID are used only for:

  • Identifying your account
  • Providing optional features that require authentication

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

5. International Use

routit is currently supported only in Ireland (beta). Geographic expansion will be included in future updates.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / EEA)

As an EEA user, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your login information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

Contact us at contact@tagora.tech for any such requests.

7. Children’s Privacy

routit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in features or compliance. Updates will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

For any questions, please contact: contact@tagora.tech
