FAQ

Some quick answers to common questions.

Do I need an account?

No. routit works without an account. Optional login is only used to provide convenience features and stores minimal information (email + UID).

Where are my routes stored?

In this beta, routes are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers.

Which countries are supported?

The app is currently focused on Ireland. Support for more regions will be rolled out gradually.
