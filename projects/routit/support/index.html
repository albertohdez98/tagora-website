Frequently Asked Questions

Is an account required?

No. You can use Routit without creating an account.

Why is route generation limited to Ireland?

Routit is currently in beta, and route generation is available in Ireland only. More countries will be added in the future.

How is my location used?

Your location is used only to generate nearby routes and is processed locally on your device.
